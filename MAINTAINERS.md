## Becoming a maintainer

See [GOVERNANCE.md § "Becoming a maintainer"](GOVERNANCE.md#becoming-a-maintainer).
The short version: sustained, high-quality contributions across multiple
releases, followed by an invitation from the current maintainer. This
list grows by invitation, not by self-nomination.
